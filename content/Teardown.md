---
title: "Teardown"
---
Teardown is the act of breaking something down into the atomic components. Note that these atomic components are NOT [Atomic Idea](Atomic%20Idea.md)s.

Teardown is followed by a [Rebuild](Rebuild.md) to ensure that we understand the [Unity and Complexity](Unity%20and%20Complexity.md) of a topic.
